# Product Guidelines

## Brand Messaging

**Formal and Technical:** Messaging should emphasize precision, data accuracy, and advanced financial concepts. The language used should reflect the sophisticated nature of high-speed trading and the target audience of experienced traders and quantitative analysts. Avoid overly simplistic or casual language. Focus on the technical capabilities, reliability, and performance of the platform.

## Visual Identity

**Clean and Functional:** The visual design should be clean, functional, and professional. Prioritize readability and efficient display of data. Use a palette that conveys seriousness and reliability, with clear distinctions for different types of information (e.g., market data, order status, alerts). Avoid overly decorative or distracting elements.

## Communication Style

**Direct and Informative:** All communications, including UI text, documentation, and error messages, should be direct, concise, and informative. Provide necessary details without jargon where possible, but do not shy away from technical terms when accuracy requires it. Error messages should be clear, actionable, and helpful for a technically proficient user base.

## User Experience (UX) Principles

**Efficiency and Control:** The UX should be optimized for efficiency, allowing users to quickly access critical information and execute actions with minimal friction. Provide users with a high degree of control over their trading activities and system configurations. Ensure that complex data is presented in an organized and intuitive manner.
